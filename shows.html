TV SHOW REVIEWS

Breaking Bad

A masterfully written crime drama that follows a chemistry teacher’s descent into the drug world. Brilliant performances and tight storytelling make it one of the most critically acclaimed series ever.

Game Of Thrones

An epic fantasy saga with rich world-building, political intrigue, and iconic characters. Though its final season divided fans, the journey up to that point is gripping and often breathtaking.

Chernobyl

A chilling, expertly crafted miniseries that dramatizes the 1986 nuclear disaster. It’s haunting, deeply human, and a powerful critique of bureaucracy and misinformation.

The Walking Dead

A post-apocalyptic survival drama that starts strong with character-driven storytelling and moral dilemmas. It loses momentum in later seasons but remains a staple in zombie TV history.

  • Full Review

Peaky Blinders

A stylish, gritty British crime drama centered on the Shelby crime family. Cillian Murphy leads a sharp cast, and the show’s atmosphere, music, and intensity keep it compelling throughout.

Shogun

A visually stunning and culturally immersive adaptation of the classic novel. Rich in political drama and historical depth, with standout performances and meticulous detail in its portrayal of feudal Japan.
